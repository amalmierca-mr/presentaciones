04 — The Format

Play
The
Brief.

One real brief. Three competing international teams. 1.5 days to create and produce a Twitch-first campaign.

The hackathon is not training. It's real production under real pressure.
01
The Brief
Real Telefónica brief — a client with a natural fit for gaming and Twitch
Twitch as the primary campaign channel
Production parameters and budget defined from day one
Amazon onboarding session to contextualise the ecosystem
02
The Teams
3 Havas Play teams: Spain, Germany, France and UK
Talent mix: strategists, creatives and gaming specialists
Internal competition with incentives for the winning team
Campaign credit + gaming/Twitch prizes for the winner
03
The Output
Three complete, modular Twitch-first campaigns
Pitch to jury: Telefónica, Havas Play leadership and Amazon
Winning campaign produced and activated on Twitch
Additional media budget contributed by Amazon for the winner
Replicable format
Once the model is proven, the hackathon rolls out to other countries and clients across the global Havas Play network.
05 — The Event

Madrid.
Distrito
Telefónica.

1.5 intensive days at Telefónica's headquarters in Madrid. Creation, pitching and jury decision in a single creative sprint.

Day 1 — Morning: Brief & Immersion
Telefónica brief presented to all three teams. Amazon-led onboarding session on the Twitch ecosystem. Teams receive the brief simultaneously and get to work.
Day 1 — Afternoon: Creative Sprint
Teams work in parallel throughout the afternoon. Deliverables locked by end of day. Full focus on building the Twitch-first campaign.
Day 2 — Pitches & Jury
All three teams present to the jury: Telefónica, Havas Play leadership and Amazon. Decision and winner announcement delivered live.
Post-Event: Production & Activation
The winning campaign enters production immediately with committed budget. Live activation on Twitch with Amazon's media boost.
